About Me

What do I do?

I'm a gardening coach, mentor and designer with a wealth of experience of working with people of all ages. I specialize in teaching people how to look after their gardens and group activities with the elderly and people suffering from illness.

How do I do it?

I use my friendly personality and my love of gardening to encourage and guide people to broaden their own gardening experience. I like to create a fun, yet informative atmosphere during group activities.

Why hire me?

With my knowledge and enthusiasm of the outdoors I work quickly to build up a rapport with my clients. I am a clear and informal communicator and working together with others is my passion.
